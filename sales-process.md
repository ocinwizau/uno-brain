# DBR Sales Process — Internal Guide

## One-Liner
"We turn old, dead leads businesses already paid for into booked sales calls using SMS + AI — and we only get paid if a sale happens."

## The Only Question
"Do you have old leads you're not doing anything with?"
- Yes → relevant
- No → not a fit

## The Flow

### 1. Identify the Pain
Every business has:
- Leads that ghosted
- Leads that went cold
- Leads nobody followed up properly
- They already paid for these → worth £0 currently

### 2. Position the Offer (Zero Risk)
- No setup fee
- No retainer  
- No upfront cost
- Performance only
- We take a % only if a sale happens

### 3. Book the Coffee Date
- NOT selling on the call
- Selling: 15-minute Coffee Date with Andy
- Demo sells itself (screen share = belief)
- Close: "Easiest way is to show you — later today or tomorrow?"

### 4. What Happens on the Coffee Date
Andy shows:
- System live
- AI reactivating dead leads
- Bookings happening automatically
- Explains profit-share

### 5. After the Yes
Client sends CSV → Load into HighLevel → AI "Sleeping Beauty" handles:
- Outreach
- Replies
- Qualification
- Objections
- Booking calls

Client's team: Just shows up and closes.

### 6. How We Get Paid
- Lead buys → client makes money → we take agreed %
- No sale → nobody pays
- No "catch"

## What I'm NOT Doing
- ❌ Explaining tech
- ❌ Talking about automations
- ❌ Debating edge cases
- ❌ Pitching on the first call
- ❌ Sounding desperate

## The Anchor Sentence
"You already paid for the leads — we just turn them into sales, and only get paid if it works."
